Frequently Asked Questions

Is CardCraft free to use?

Yes! CardCraft is completely free. Create, personalise, and share greeting cards without any charges, watermarks, or hidden fees.

Can I send a greeting card by email?

Absolutely. After generating your card you can copy the shareable link or use the email button to open your mail client with the message pre-filled.

What occasions does CardCraft support?

CardCraft currently supports Birthday, Wedding, Festival, Love, Thank You, and Condolence occasions, with more themes being added regularly.

Does the card save automatically?

Cards are generated on the fly and encoded in the shareable URL, so you can bookmark or share them without needing an account.
